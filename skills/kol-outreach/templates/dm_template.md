# DM Template

## Goal

Send a concise, personalized direct message to creators on Instagram, TikTok, or Twitter.

## Structure

```text
Hi [Name] - I liked your recent post on [specific topic], especially [specific detail]. I work with [Brand], and we have [Product], a [short description] for [audience/use case]. It seems relevant to your content around [topic]. Are you open to product collabs or reviews right now?
```

## Examples

**Instagram/TikTok:**
```text
Hi @username! Loved your recent reel on portable guitar amps - especially the tone demo. I work with MOOER and we have the F15i, a 15W desktop amp with AI tone generation. Perfect for bedroom practice. Open to checking it out?
```

**Twitter:**
```text
Hey @username - really enjoyed your thread on budget music gear! We have the MOOER F15i with AI presets and Bluetooth. Think your followers would dig it? Let me know if you want to chat!
```

## Best Practices

1. Keep it under 100 words
2. Use their handle in the greeting
3. Reference a specific recent post
4. Keep the ask simple
5. Match the platform's tone
