# LinkedIn Template

## Goal

Send professional outreach messages for B2B collaborations, corporate partnerships, or professional creators.

## Structure

```text
Subject: Collaboration opportunity with [Company]

Hi [Name],

I came across your work on [topic/platform] and was impressed by [specific detail/accomplishment]. 

I'm reaching out from [Company], where we [brief company description]. We're working on [Product/Initiative], which [brief value proposition]. Given your expertise in [area], I thought you might be interested in [collaboration type: speaking, content, partnership].

Would you be open to a quick chat to explore?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Company]
[LinkedIn Profile]
```

## Example

```text
Subject: SaaS tool collaboration opportunity

Hi John,

I came across your LinkedIn articles on productivity tools for startups and was impressed by your practical approach to workflow optimization.

I'm reaching out from TechCo, where we build AI-powered project management software. Our latest tool helps teams reduce meeting time by 40% through intelligent agenda automation. Given your audience of startup founders and operators, I thought this could be valuable content for your followers.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to discuss?

Best regards,
Sarah Chen
Product Marketing Manager
TechCo
linkedin.com/in/sarahchen
```

## Best Practices

1. Keep it professional but approachable
2. Reference their professional achievements
3. Clearly state the value proposition
4. Suggest a specific next step
5. Include your LinkedIn profile link
