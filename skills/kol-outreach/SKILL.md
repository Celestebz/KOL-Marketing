# KOL Outreach - Personalized Messaging Tool

## Core Principle

Every outreach message must be personalized to the creator and their audience. Generic messages get ignored. This skill guides users to:
1. Research the creator's recent content
2. Craft personalized opening lines
3. Create platform-appropriate messages
4. Prepare follow-up sequences

## Workflow

1. **Input Collection**: Get KOL candidate list, product details, and campaign goal
2. **Research Guidance**: Define what research is needed for each candidate
3. **Message Generation**: Create personalized email/DM drafts
4. **Review & Refine**: Adjust messages for tone and platform
5. **Outreach Plan**: Create sending schedule and follow-up strategy

## Input Requirements

Before generating messages, collect:

- **KOL Candidate Information**:
  - Creator name and handle
  - Platform(s) (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, etc.)
  - Recent relevant content URLs
  - Audience demographics
  - Contact information

- **Product/Campaign Details**:
  - Product name and description
  - Key selling points
  - Campaign goal (review, demo, UGC, affiliate, etc.)
  - Offer type (free product, paid, affiliate, etc.)
  - Budget constraints

## Research Checklist

For each KOL, gather:

1. **Recent Content**: 2-3 most recent relevant posts/videos/articles
2. **Specific Details**: A unique point, style, or opinion to reference
3. **Content Format**: Their typical format (review, demo, tutorial, etc.)
4. **Past Coverage**: Similar products/topics they've covered
5. **Best Contact**: Email, website form, agency, or DM

## Message Types

### Email
Best for professional outreach, detailed pitches, or formal collaborations.

### DM (Direct Message)
Best for Instagram, TikTok, Twitter - shorter, more conversational.

### LinkedIn Message
Best for B2B, professional creators, or corporate partnerships.

## Templates

Use the templates in the `templates/` folder:
- `email_template.md` - Email outreach template
- `dm_template.md` - Direct message template
- `linkedin_template.md` - LinkedIn message template
- `outreach_plan_template.md` - Outreach tracking plan

## Message Rules

1. **Personalize**: Reference a specific recent piece of content
2. **Keep it short**: Aim for 80-150 words
3. **Match the creator**: Use language that fits their style
4. **Connect to audience**: Explain relevance to their viewers
5. **Low-friction CTA**: Ask one simple question
6. **No attachments first**: Use links instead
7. **No overpromising**: Avoid unsubstantiated claims

## Follow-Up Strategy

| Stage | Timing | Action |
|---|---|---|
| First message | Day 0 | Initial outreach |
| Follow-up 1 | Day 7-10 | Polite reminder |
| Follow-up 2 | Day 14-15 | Additional value or closing |
| Close | Day 21 | Archive or escalate |

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

| Platform | Message Length | Best Time | Tone |
|---|---|---|---|
| Email | 80-150 words | Work hours | Professional |
| Instagram DM | 50-80 words | Afternoon | Friendly, conversational |
| TikTok DM | 50-80 words | Evening | Casual, enthusiastic |
| LinkedIn | 100-120 words | Work hours | Professional |
| Twitter | <280 chars | Anytime | Concise |

## Campaign Tracking

Create an outreach tracking sheet with:
- KOL Name
- Platform
- Contact Info
- Message Sent Date
- Follow-up Status
- Response
- Outcome

## Output

- Personalized message drafts for each KOL
- Outreach schedule
- Tracking template
- Follow-up reminders
