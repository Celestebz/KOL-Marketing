# Outreach Message Guidelines

## Goal

Prompt the creator/KOL to reply with interest in learning about, trying, reviewing, demonstrating, or collaborating around the product.

## Rules

1. **Personalize:** Reference a specific recent post, video, article, podcast, or point they made.
2. **Keep it short:** Aim for 80-150 words unless the category requires technical detail.
3. **Match the creator:** Use language that fits their platform and content style.
4. **Connect to their audience:** Explain why the product is relevant to their viewers/followers/readers.
5. **Use a low-friction CTA:** Ask one simple question.
6. **Avoid attachments first:** Use a product link or short one-liner instead.
7. **Do not overpromise:** Avoid claims that need regulatory, medical, financial, or technical proof unless verified.

## CTA Examples

Choose based on campaign goal:

- "Would you be interested in taking a look?"
- "Can I send you more details?"
- "Are you open to reviewing products in this category this month?"
- "Would you be interested in trying it and sharing honest feedback?"
- "Is this something your audience would find useful?"
- "Who handles partnerships for your channel?"

## Email Structure

```text
Subject: Quick question about [specific topic/content]

Hi [Name],

I saw your recent [video/post/article] on [topic] and liked your point about [specific detail]. [One sentence showing real context.]

I'm reaching out from [Company] about [Product Name], a [one-sentence product description]. I think it could fit your audience because [creator/audience-specific reason].

Would you be interested in [trying it / reviewing it / seeing more details]?

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

## DM Structure

```text
Hi [Name] - I liked your recent post on [specific topic], especially [specific detail]. I work with [Brand], and we have [Product], a [short description] for [audience/use case]. It seems relevant to your content around [topic]. Are you open to product collabs or reviews right now?
```

## Research Required Before Outreach

For each KOL, find:

- Their 2-3 most recent relevant posts/videos/articles.
- A specific point or style detail to reference.
- Their typical content format.
- Similar products/topics they have covered.
- Best contact route: email, website form, agency, or DM.

## Offer Guidance

Use the offer that fits the product and creator tier:

- Free product/sample for honest feedback.
- Paid sponsorship.
- Affiliate/commission.
- Discount code for audience.
- Early access or beta access.
- Expert briefing or founder interview.
- UGC package.

Do not assume one offer works for all categories.

## Follow-Up

If no response after 7-10 days:

- Send one polite follow-up.
- Reference the original message.
- Keep it under 50 words.
- Do not pressure for a timeline.

## What Not To Do

- Send generic mass messages.
- Mention specific payment amounts in the first message unless the campaign requires it.
- Ask for exclusivity upfront.
- Attach large files or press kits.
- Make unverified product claims.
- Copy another creator's personalization.
